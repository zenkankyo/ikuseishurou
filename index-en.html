Background and Urgency of the Issue

The Ikusei-Shuro system, newly introduced as a replacement for the Technical Intern Training Program, contains serious challenges. Workers face unstable employment conditions, lack of support, and a system that prioritizes companies over human rights.

We aim to raise awareness of these problems and call for improvement to build a truly fair labor system.

Our Objectives

  • Highlight the voices of workers on the ground
  • Communicate concerns to society and government
  • Promote concrete measures to protect human rights
