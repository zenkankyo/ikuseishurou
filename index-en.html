Our Mission

The National Association of Supervising Organizations (NASO) is dedicated to ensuring fair and transparent management of foreign workers in Japan. We work to protect human rights, improve working conditions, and build trust between companies and trainees.

Key Activities

  • Monitoring and supervising training programs
  • Supporting both trainees and companies
  • Providing consultation on laws and systems
  • Promoting public awareness of related issues
